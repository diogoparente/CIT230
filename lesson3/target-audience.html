Audience

The target audience of the website is:

  • Who: Any person living or not in the cities the website is related to. People planning to visit any of these cities.
  • Device: Any device, can be a cellphone, tablet, or a computer.
  • Motivation: Know the time to do an activity, what clothes to use, take an umbrella or not when leaving home, plan to go outside or stay at home.

Persona 1

John Silva

Personal information: 17 years old. High School student, studying Economics.

Reason to use the website: Plan his daily activities, like going to his internship and High School course.

Professional: Few months as an internship in an office.

Devices usage: Smartphone, tablet, smartwatch, desktop computer at school, and a personal computer. All of them with internet access.

Persona 2

May Soares

Personal information: 73 years old. Retired, worked for a local industry during her entire life.

Reason to use the website: Know the current weather conditions to plan her daily activities

Professional: Many diferent activities, like cleaning, gardening, buying supplies.

Devices usage: Has access to the internet using a computer at home.

Scenarios

  • Will it rain tomorrow?
  • Do I need to carry my umbrella today?
  • What will be the minimun and maximun temperatures for this afternoon.
  • Will it be sunny at the beach?
  • Is there any alert for a bad weather situation?
  • How will be the weather next weekend?
  • Should I carry an additional jacket today?
