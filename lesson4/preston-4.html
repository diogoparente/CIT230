Site Name

This website will be called The Weather for You. The website will be a source of information about the weather condition in the areas listed.

Site Purpose

The main purpose of the TheWeather4You.com website is to notify its users about the weather condition in the locations of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven.

The weather forecast will be given for the current and next few days.

The information displayed will be the temperature, sun and clouds condition, wind speed and direction, rain or snow precipitation, and any important alert.

Domain Name

The domain for the "The Weather for You" website will be www.theweather4you.com.
